Run ImageMaid in move mode?
Move mode renames and relocates Plex metadata images into the ImageMaid Restore folder.
Quickstart will show the exact restore folder before you run.
Stop ImageMaid?
Are you sure you want to stop the current ImageMaid run? This will terminate the process and clear the PID.
ImageMaid removes Plex poster and background files that were uploaded by you or by other tools, but are no longer in use.
Modes: report previews unused images, move sends them to the ImageMaid Restore folder, restore puts those images back into Plex, clear empties the ImageMaid Restore folder, remove is effectively move and clear in one step so you cannot use restore afterward, and nothing skips image cleanup.
Start with report if you want to review what ImageMaid would act on before choosing another mode.
Reference: ImageMaid wiki
